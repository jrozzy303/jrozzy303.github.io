PENTATNONIX (A CAPELLA/POP)


The Pentatonix are a five member A capella group, made up of:

Scott Hoying (Baritone)

With just their voices, this musical group can create such beautiful harmonies. If you want to find out more, Go check out: http://ptxofficial.com
